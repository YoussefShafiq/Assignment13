Iron Man

Real name: tony stark

First Appearance: Tales of Suspense #39 (1963)

Creators: Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, Don Heck, and Jack Kirby

Powers/Abilities: Genius-level intellect, expert engineer and businessman, powered armor suit granting superhuman strength, flight, energy blasts, and an array of weapons and gadgets.

Thor

Real name: Thor Odinson

First Appearance: Journey into Mystery #83 (1962)

Creators: Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, and Jack Kirby

Powers/Abilities: Superhuman strength, speed, durability, and longevity; weather manipulation; ability to fly using Mjolnir; energy projection; ability to travel between dimensions.

Captin America

Real name: Steve Rogers

First Appearance: Captain America Comics #1 (1941)

Creators: Joe Simon and Jack Kirby

Powers/Abilities: Peak human physical condition, master martial artist and hand-to-hand combatant, expert tactician, wielding an indestructible shield.

Hulk

Real name: Bruce Banner

First Appearance: The Incredible Hulk #1 (1962)

Creators: Stan Lee and Jack Kirby

Powers/Abilities: Superhuman strength, durability, and regeneration, transforming into the Hulk when angry or stressed.

Avengers

First Appearance: The Avengers #1 (1963)

Creators: Stan Lee and Jack Kirby

Original Team Members: Iron Man, Thor, Hulk, Ant-Man, and Wasp.

Purpose: To combat threats that no single hero can handle alone.

Stark Industries

Owner: tony stark

First Appearance: Tales of Suspense #40 (1963)

Industry: Multinational industrial company primarily known for advanced weaponry and technology.

Significance: Stark Industries is central to Tony Stark’s identity as Iron Man, funding his technological advancements and the Avengers. The company shifts focus from weapons manufacturing to clean energy and technology under Tony’s guidance.
